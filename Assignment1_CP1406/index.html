DIGGER'S BEACH VOLLEYBALL

Who can play ?

Beach volleyball is suitable for people of all ages, and men and women alike. Anyone considering starting a new sport or exercise program should see a doctor first. Volleyball is a sport that is safe, fun and does not require extreme fitness. Since you don’t have to move around the court too fast, it’s great for people who want a low-impact sport that will help them get fit without pushing them to their limit.
